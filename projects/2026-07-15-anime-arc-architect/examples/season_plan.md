# Neon Paper Cranes — Season Architecture

In the floating city of Hoshima, memories can be folded into paper familiars. When whole neighborhoods begin forgetting the same night, a rooftop courier and a disgraced archivist must trace the missing memories before the city votes to erase its own past.
